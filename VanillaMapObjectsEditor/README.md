# Vanilla Map Objects Editor
Advanced Map Objects from vanilla maps. All new objects can be found in the Advanced category

### Variable Box
A physics box with customizable mass. Comes in a normal and background variant.
Mass values for boxes in vanilla:
- 2x2: 20 mass
- 1x12: 40 mass
- 2x6: 60 mass
- 5x5: 100 mass

### Dynamic Saw
A physics saw with customizable mass. In vanilla, they typically have a scale of 7.5x7.5 and a mass of 100

### Destructible Box
A destructible box with customiable health and mass. In vanilla, they always have 50 health and 20 mass, and are scaled at 1.3x1.3

### Moving Platform
A physics platform that moves between several offsets. 
- **Spring** controls the movement speed and force. 
- **Time At Position** controls how long it stays at each position
- **Positions** are all offsets from the object's original position.
For best results, do not copy/paste or animate this object.

### Wrecking Ball
A physics ball with customizable mass. In vanilla, they have 500 mass and are scaled at 5x5

### Smasher
Smashers from "Factory 1". Initial delay is customizable.
